What can you do with FUNDME ?

It is not an overemphasized outcry that education in Africa is debilitating with millions of school age children out of school while portions of the ones that can attend, lack basic school needs. In our research, we discovered people are as willing to give to the cause almost as people are to accept it but the pending issues of transparency and fraud limits the hands of givers.

Core Purpose

FUNDME moves to establish itself as a donation application in Africa connecting helpers to the people in need. It aims to address pressing obstacles in online donation in Africa such as; creating a database of donors and beneficiaries to eradicate the stress of looking for one on both parts. Simplify and digitize the process of donation.
